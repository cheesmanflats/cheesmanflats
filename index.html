Exemption Compliance with House Bill 23-1099:

Application Fee and PTSR Policies

In accordance with House Bill 23-1099, Cheesman Flats and the landlords we represent are exempt from the requirements of subsections (1.5)(a)-(1.5)(e) of Colorado Revised Statutes, Section 38-12-904 (1.5). This exemption applies because we charge only one application fee for applicants who will be cohabitating and refund this fee if the lease is not signed. Therefore, we are not required to accept Portable Tenant Screening Reports (PTSR) from prospective tenants, as per the exemption criteria outlined in subsection (1.5)(f).

History

Brownstone Investments LLC, dba Cheesman Flats, has built a legacy over the past three decades by managing a curated collection of vintage homes. These single-family homes, elegantly transformed into premium flats, are located just west of the esteemed Cheesman Park, offering residents both charm and convenience.

Our commitment to excellence in property management has earned us a reputation as the go-to choice for individuals and families seeking high-quality living in the vibrant community adjacent to Cheesman Park. We pride ourselves on providing unparalleled tenant experiences through professionalism, transparency, and exceptional service.

As we look to the future, we are excited to continue offering outstanding living experiences while preserving the unique character of our properties and meeting the evolving needs of our residents with unwavering excellence and care.
