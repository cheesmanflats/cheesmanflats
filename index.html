Application Fee & PTSR Policy

In accordance with House Bill 23-1099, Brownstone Investments LLC charges only one application fee for co-applicants and refunds it if a lease is not signed. Therefore, we are exempt from accepting Portable Tenant Screening Reports (PTSR).
