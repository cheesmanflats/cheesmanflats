Exemption Compliance with House Bill 23-1099: Application Fee and PTSR Policies

In accordance with House Bill 23-1099, Brownstone Investments LLC and the landlords we represent are exempt from the requirements of subsections (1.5)(a)-(1.5)(e) of Colorado Revised Statutes, Section 38-12-904 (1.5). This exemption applies because we charge only one application fee for applicants who will be cohabitating and refund this fee if the lease is not signed. Therefore, we are not required to accept Portable Tenant Screening Reports (PTSR) from prospective tenants, as per the exemption criteria outlined in subsection (1.5)(f).

Brownstone Investments History

Brownstone Investments LLC, warmly known as Brownstone Leasing, has meticulously cultivated a prestigious history that spans three decades. This history is characterized by the management of a carefully selected collection of historic homes that have been elegantly transformed into premium apartments. These unique properties are ideally situated just west of the highly esteemed Cheesman Park, making them a coveted location for residents seeking both charm and convenience.

Throughout these years, our unwavering dedication has been to ensure that each tenant receives unparalleled property management services, fostering a living experience that is second to none. These principles have not only elevated our standing in the property management industry but have also solidified our reputation as the go-to choice for individuals and families seeking high-quality housing in the dynamic and vibrant community adjacent to Cheesman Park.

We take immense pride in our extensive history and the legacy we have built, a testament to our enduring commitment to excellence and our passion for serving our community. As we look to the future, we are filled with enthusiasm for the opportunities that lie ahead, continuing our mission to provide outstanding living experiences, preserve the unique character of our properties, and meet the evolving needs of our residents with unwavering excellence and care.
